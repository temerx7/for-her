My dearest love,

There are moments in life that stop time — the first time I saw you smile, the first time you laughed so hard you couldn't breathe, the first time I realised I was completely, helplessly in love with you.

You are the most extraordinary person I have ever known. Not because you're perfect — but because every beautiful, imperfect part of you is something I want to know more deeply every single day.

On this day, I don't just want to celebrate you as a woman — I want to celebrate every version of you. The strong one, the soft one, the silly one, the brave one who keeps choosing love even when it's scary.

Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for every ordinary Tuesday and every extraordinary moment in between. I would walk every road again just to find you at the end of it.

This is my promise: I will keep showing up. Always.

Forever yours 💕
